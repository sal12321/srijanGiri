About the Game

Set on a hostile alien planet, the game puts players in the role of a survivor navigating a world filled with deadly creatures and complex alliances. Players explore a dangerous alien world, tame and battle dinosaurs, craft weapons, and build relationships with a cast of unique characters.

The game combines fast-paced combat with narrative-driven progression and romance systems — a cohesive blend of survival mechanics, RPG progression, and relationship-building.

Key Learnings

L1

Modular Architecture at Scale

Designing a data-driven combat framework that supports unlimited weapon types, combo chains, and AI archetypes without coupling systems together — enabling designers to iterate without touching code.

L2

Stabilising a Live Codebase

Integrating a rebuilt combat system into an unstable, complex existing codebase without breaking core gameplay. Learned to isolate dependencies, write defensive interfaces, and test incrementally.

L3

GAS-Inspired Action Architecture

Encapsulating actions as independent objects with a centralised management component allows both player input and AI behaviour trees to drive execution through the same pipeline cleanly.

L4

AI Coordination & Group Awareness

Designing group AI that coordinates attacks — controlling how many enemies engage simultaneously — fundamentally changes how combat feels. Preventing AI pile-ons creates fairness and telegraphing.

03

Personal Contribution

When I joined the project, the existing combat system was unstable and lacked a scalable structure. Within a tight timeline of approximately 2 months, I rebuilt the combat system from the ground up and integrated a modular, data-driven combat framework.

I

Player Combat Mechanics

  • Designed and implemented a responsive melee combat system
  • Light and heavy attack combos
  • Special abilities driven by an adrenaline resource system
  • Blocking mechanics with feedback and window states
  • Dodge system with configurable invincibility frames (i-frames)
  • Upgrade-friendly architecture to support progression systems

Player melee combo system with adrenaline abilities

II

Enemy AI Systems

Enemy perception, group aggro & attack coordination

  • Developed intelligent AI using perception systems (sight, sound, damage detection)
  • Implemented group awareness — enemies alert nearby allies on detection
  • Designed attack coordination logic controlling how many enemies engage simultaneously
  • Built flexible AI behaviour supporting multiple attack patterns and archetypes
  • Implemented disengagement logic — AI drops aggro when player exits range
III

Companion AI

  • Created a fully functional friendly AI companion system
  • Companion follows the player, assists in combat, and dynamically engages hostile targets
  • Integrated seamlessly with both player and enemy combat loops

Companion AI following, engaging & assisting player

IV

System Architecture & Integration

Modular combat framework — data-driven weapon & combo system

  • Built and integrated a modular combat framework plugin
  • Data-driven system enabling unlimited weapon types, flexible combo chains, scalable AI archetypes
  • Action-based architecture inspired by UE5's Gameplay Ability System (GAS) — actions as independent objects with a centralised management component
  • Supports both player input and AI-driven execution (via behaviour trees)
  • Successfully integrated into an unoptimised and complex existing codebase without breaking core gameplay
